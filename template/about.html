OUR STORY

For generations, Roof Tech LLC has been a trusted name in commercial roofing. What began as a small, family-owned business has evolved into one of Wisconsin's most reliable roofing contractors, with a reputation built on quality, dependability, and superior customer service.

Roof Tech was founded by Michael Yurkovich, a skilled tradesman with a vision to create a roofing company that prioritized customer satisfaction above all else. Michael's commitment to excellence and his hands-on approach to every project quickly set Roof Tech apart from the competition.

Since the beginning of Roof Tech LLC, we have brought a new perspective to our clients' roofing needs. We don't just patch problems; we create long-term solutions that protect your investment and provide peace of mind for years to come.

Today, Roof Tech LLC continues to uphold the values that Michael established from the start. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology and materials, ensuring that every roof we install or repair meets the highest standards of quality and durability.
